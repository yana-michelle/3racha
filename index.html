Stray Kids

Stray Kids is a Kpop group under the label JYP Entertainment

The group consists of eight members

  • Bang Chan
  • Lee Know
  • Felix
  • Changbin
  • Han
  • Hyunjin
  • Seungmin
  • I.N
  • Woojin (EX member)


Bang Chan, also known by his English name Christopher Bang, is not only the leader of Stray Kids but also The main rapper and vocalist. Chan also produces and writes almost all of Stray Kids' songs. He was born in South Korea but moved to Sydney, Australia at the age of one. He soon moved to South Korea to begin his training, which lasted seven years. Throughout those seven years he hand picked each member of Stray Kids, something very rare in the Kpop industry.



Lee Know, birth name Lee Minho, is the main dancer, rapper, and vocalist. Born inGimpo, South Korea, Minho had fallen in love with dance in middle school. He was even was a back up dancer for the very famous group BTS during their Japan tour. Minho was actually cut from the group before debuting, shown in the survival show Stray Kids participated in before debut. He was brought back weeks before their debut and was able to fulfill his dream of debuting with his closest friends.


Felix, full name Lee Felix, was also born in Sydney, Austraila. He is the main dancer, lead rapper, and visual of the group. When Felix moved to South Korea he did not know any Korean, which had him resort to hand signs and asking Chan to translate for him. Felix was also cut from the group before debut but was also brought back at the same time as Minho was.



Seo Changbin is the group's main rapper, vocalist, and produces moany of their songs as well. He was born in Yongin, South Korea
