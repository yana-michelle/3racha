Stray Kids

Stray Kids is a Kpop group under the label JYP Entertainment

The group consists of eight members

  • Bang Chan
  • Lee Know
  • Felix
  • Changbin
  • Han
  • Hyunjin
  • Seungmin
  • I.N

Bang Chan, also known by his English name Christopher Bang, is not only the leader of Stray Kids but also The main rapper and vocalist. Chan also produces and writes almost all of Stray Kids' songs. He was born in South Korea but moved to Australia at the age of one. He renounced his Korean nationality and spent his childhood in Australia. He tried out for JYP at the age of ten and was accepted. He moved to South Korea to begin his training. He spent nine years in training before finally debuting with the rest of Stray Kids.



Lee Know, birth name Lee Minho, is the main dancer of stray kids as well as a vocalist. He was originally a back up dancer for the very famous group BTS. Minho was actually cut from the group before debuting, shown in the survival show Stray Kids participated in before debut. He was brought back weeks before their debut
